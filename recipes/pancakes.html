Blueberry Flax Pancakes

A whole and Healthy Pancake Mixture with Delicious Blueberries

Set a non-stick skillet over medium heat. In a medium bowl stir the pancakes mix and flax seed meal, and in a separate bowl whisk the eggs an milk together. Then pour the mixtures together and stir until moistened.

Spoon 1/4 cupfuls of batter onto the hot skillet. Sprinkle with as many blueberries as desired. Cook until bubbles appear on the surface, then flip and cook until browned on the other side.

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ cups dry pancake mix
  • ½ cup flax seed meal
  • 1 cup skim milk
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup fresh or thawed frozen blueberries

Steps

  1. Set a nonstick skillet over medium heat.
  2. In a medium bowl, stir together the pancake mix and flax seed meal.
  3. In a separate bowl or measuring cup, whisk together the milk and eggs. Then pour the liquid into the dry ingredients, and stir just until moistened.
  4. Spoon 1/4 cupfuls of batter onto the hot skillet and sprinkle with as many blueberries as desired.
  5. Cook until bubbles appear on the surface, then flip and cook until browned on the other side.
